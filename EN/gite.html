« Do not worry about a thing, we offer you all the comfort you need to spend a great time in vacation. »

Our B&B is a 45m² accommodation with a private entrance and can host up to 5 persons: a bright and spacious room (bed for 2), a living room with a spare bed for one, a mezzanine easily reachable thanks to a miller’s ladder.

In the bathroom, you will find an Italian shower (floor level), toilet and a washing-machine.

In the kitchen, you will find a fridge equipped with a freezer, a glass-ceramic hob, an electric oven, a micro-waves, a dishwasher and Tassimo coffee-machine.

A TV is available in the living room. In the bathroom, you will find an Italian shower (floor level), toilet and a washing-machine.

You will also have access to a car-park, a private equipped patio and a garden.
